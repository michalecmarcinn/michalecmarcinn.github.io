About us


Our vision

We envision a new incarnation of modern flamenco dance,
in which the old and the new are fused to create dance,
combining the dramatic vision and integrity of the great
modern dance masterpieces, with the creativity and energy,and we will give form
this through our dances classes and
the ceaseless effort of our professional teachers.


Our mission

We will promote couples flamenco dancing in a new and
exciting way, and expand our business, in order to promote
our healthy activities to people in the copenhagen area.


Who are we

Our Step One school consists of a team of dedicated teachers,
that through the dance of Flamenco, provides healthy exercise
to our customers, while providing teambuilding partner dance
sessions, that will bring back that spark of intimacy and joy
between couples.
Experience Flamenco in a new way!
